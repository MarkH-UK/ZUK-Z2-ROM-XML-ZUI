Welcome ZUK FANS EUROPE MULTI LANGUAGE PROJECT

Our project aims to raise awareness of ZUK and ZUI, a product made by Lenovo Mobile Groups new company, ZUK. With Chinese phones more popular than ever, consumers are importing phones and are facing the big challenge of no localisation inside the core OS. At ZUKfans.eu we aim to change this by starting our own independent multi language ROM project.
